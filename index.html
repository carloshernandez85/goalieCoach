What we work on

A quick listing of what we teach to take you or your goalie to the next level. We teach the fundaments, that way each goalie will never be outpaced.

Edge Control

We teach an emphasis on skating - ever heard the goalie must be the best skater on the team? Well, that saying holds true. This will take your game to the moon!

Angle Training

Learn how to proper positioning (with respect to the play) and cover all option available. You can't trust your D 100%, best to always be prepared and anticipating with your body/posture properly aligned.

Proper Workout

Learn how to properly take care of your body. Playing goalie requires different muscle group firing than skating out, we teach you proper preventative measures against injuries.

How To Be A Good Teammate

Nobody wants to be teammates with a scumbag. Soft skills are very important, learn how to play well with others. They're your teammates, when things get rough, they're going to back you up.

How To Communicate & Control Plays

We teach how to direct traffic for the extra man, p kills and transition play. Communication is a huge benefit at high level hockey, don't be THAT goalie that never helps his teammates.

Constant Improvement

Never be comfortable. In fact, get used to being uncomfortable and honing your skills. With the foundations we teach, your game will never be the same.
